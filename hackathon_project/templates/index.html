Our Services

Skill-Based Profile Creation

Users can create detailed profiles showcasing their skills, similar to professional networking sites like LinkedIn. This allows potential employers to gain a clear understanding of the user’s abilities.

Task Assignment

The platform provides users with tasks, tests, and projects that are related to their skills. This allows users to apply their skills in a practical setting and gain valuable experience.

Performance Evaluation

An intelligent algorithm checks the correctness of the tasks, detects cheating, and identifies plagiarism. This ensures that the work submitted by users is original and accurately reflects their abilities.

Recognition of Achievement

Users are awarded badges and remarks based on their performance on the tasks. This provides a visual representation of the user’s skills and achievements.

Company-Student Matching

The platform recommends companies to students based on their skills, and vice versa. This ensures that users are matched with companies that are interested in their specific skill set.

Suitability for Research-Based Internships

The platform is particularly beneficial for students seeking research-based internships, where specific technical competencies and innovative thinking are highly valued.

About Us

Welcome to our platform, a unique space designed to bridge the gap between students and potential internship providers. We understand the challenges students face in securing internships that provide real-world experience and enhance employability. Our platform is designed to address these issues innovatively. We help students create detailed skill-based profiles, similar to professional networking sites like LinkedIn, but with a twist. Our platform provides students with tasks, tests, and projects related to their skills. An intelligent algorithm checks the correctness, detects cheating, and identifies plagiarism in these tasks. The performance of the students on these tasks is recognized through badges and remarks, providing companies with a more accurate representation of the student’s abilities. Furthermore, our platform recommends companies to students based on their skills and vice versa. Our solution is particularly beneficial for students seeking research-based internships, where specific technical competencies and innovative thinking are highly valued. We aim to provide a comprehensive approach to matching students with internships based on verified skills and performance on relevant tasks. Join us today and take a step towards a brighter future!
